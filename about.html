Our Story

How we became the leading second-hand car marketplace

2015

The Beginning

AutoDeals was founded with a simple vision: to create a transparent and trustworthy platform for buying and selling second-hand cars. We started with just 5 cars and a small team of passionate automotive enthusiasts.

2017

Rapid Growth

By 2017, we had expanded our inventory to over 500 cars and established partnerships with certified dealers across the region. Our commitment to quality and customer service began to set us apart.

2019

Digital Innovation

We launched our comprehensive online platform, making it easier than ever for customers to browse, compare, and purchase vehicles from the comfort of their homes.

2021

Quality Assurance

Introduced our rigorous 150-point inspection process, ensuring every vehicle meets our high standards for safety, reliability, and performance.

2024

Market Leader

Today, AutoDeals is recognized as one of the leading second-hand car marketplaces, with over 10,000 successful transactions and a customer satisfaction rate of 98%.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Trust & Transparency

We believe in complete honesty about every vehicle's condition, history, and pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises - just transparent dealings.

Quality Assurance

Every car undergoes our comprehensive inspection process. We only list vehicles that meet our strict quality and safety standards.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We're committed to providing exceptional service and support throughout your car buying journey.

Reliability

Innovation
