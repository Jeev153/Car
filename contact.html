Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive by calling us at +1 (555) 123-4567, filling out the contact form above, or visiting our showroom during business hours. We recommend scheduling in advance to ensure the vehicle is available.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we work with multiple financing partners to offer competitive rates and flexible terms. Our finance team can help you find the best option based on your credit profile and budget.

What warranty do you provide?

All our vehicles come with a comprehensive warranty that varies by age and mileage. Most cars include a 3-month/3,000-mile warranty covering major components. Extended warranty options are also available.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

We accept trade-ins and will provide you with a fair market value assessment. The trade-in value can be applied toward your purchase, reducing the amount you need to finance.

How do you inspect your vehicles?

Every vehicle undergoes our comprehensive 150-point inspection covering engine, transmission, brakes, electrical systems, and more. We also provide a detailed inspection report with each vehicle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, certified checks, bank transfers, and financing through our approved lenders. Credit cards are accepted for deposits and down payments up to $5,000.
